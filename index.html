Magitech Workshop

The train pulls into Millbrook station as the morning sun breaks through the clouds. You step onto the platform with a single bag and a letter from a solicitor.

Your uncle - a man you barely knew - has left you his workshop. The place is filled with strange devices and half-finished notes about something called "aether." With nothing else going on in your life, this feels like an opportunity for a new start.

As you settle in, you'll discover the devices can reshape the human body in remarkable ways - and that the townsfolk of Millbrook have desires of their own they might share with someone they trust.

How to Play

Explore the town, get to know its people, and uncover your uncle's work.

Day Phases Each day has three phases: Morning, Afternoon, and Evening. Actions take time, advancing the phase.
Aether Magical energy stored in the crystal cube. Devices draw 2 to increase stats. The cube passively absorbs ambient aether each morning based on how many townsfolk trust you. Infused items from around town also replenish it.
Coin Money earned by completing requests at the workshop board. Spend it at shops around town.
Desire Tracker Check the desire tracker for hints about what each townsfolk secretly wants - or what might be preventing them from opening up to you.
